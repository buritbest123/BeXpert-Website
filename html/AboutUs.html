About US


BeXprt is an online portfolio that allows people to create their portfolios online. Tell about themselves, demonstrate their skills, and include their experience, certificates, and CVs/resumes for each position they want to work in to let the company see them. It is also a place for a company to find the best candidate for a job in their company.

How to Use BeXpert

For Company

  • Login or Register to Bexpert
  • Search for the position your company wants (search by skills)
  • Choose the right person for your organization by going to their profile for more information and CV/Resume
  • Contact them using the contact provided

For User

  • Login or Register to Bexpert
  • Create a portfolio by adding your personal information (Fullname, Nickname, Birthday, Email, Contact Number)
  • Add Skills, Certificates, CV/Resume and also the position you're interested in
  • Wait for the Company to contact you via given contact
